This demo demonstrates separating the UI and UX of how Ionic Framework is architected to enable the ability to have the "UX" based on the platform (iOS or Android), but have a custom appearance (UI).
The "OS" theme is the only theme that consumes the global design tokens. It is the "customizable" theme, where the iOS and MD modes offer limited customization.
